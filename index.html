The Black Plague 1348

There are many frequently occurring plagues in 15th-16th century Florence. Bubonic plagues, like the Black Death, whose symptoms were fever, weakness, and blood loss. It results in the death of more than 20% of Florence’s inhabitants.

Colonizations XIX Century

New territories are being discovered daily and European powers become greedy for lands and precious materials. You have conquered this theme without colonizing anyone, for now.

Wall Street Crash of 1929

Welcome to the Economic Depression! The economy collapses and trade fails. Alcohol is prohibited so if we catch you drinking….

Cold War 1947-1991

Brrr this war is really cold and scheming like a chess game. During WWII USA and URSS were allies, however, their relationship was tense. President Reagan fights communism till its end. The Berlin wall finally falls, and the Cold War ends.

Alien Invasion 2050+

It’s 2053. Pollution has destroyed the environment, men caused their own extinction. Earth has become … a new world. The richest people fled to Mars by shooting missiles, what was planet Earth good for? Intergalactic snails have found a good home, by eating corpses and toxic materials, their radioactivity even increased. Are you one of the remaining humans? Eat or be eaten!
